Our story

Cooking with fire, season and soul

Saffron & Sage began as a tiny supper club and grew into a neighbourhood favourite. Our kitchen works with local farms, a wood-fired hearth, and a simple rule: let great ingredients do the talking.

  • 🔥
    Wood-fired everything

    Steaks, vegetables and breads kissed by open flame.

  • 🌿
    Local & seasonal

    Menus that change with the harvest, never against it.

  • 🍷
    A cellar worth exploring

    150+ labels hand-picked by our sommelier.
